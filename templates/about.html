About this project

This is the Final Project For CS50 2022.

Reason for choosing the project idea,
is my great love for time management and productivity.

I wanted to make it easier for people, collect most of the tools they may need to manage their time, and develop themselves in one application, so that each person would be able to control his life, and use his time in creativity.

Thanks to cs50 team and Harvard University.
I want to thank all the CS50 team, and David J Malan and Brian Yu for their hard work.
Now, I was able to implement this project, and present it as a final project, thanks to you.
